Platform

The powerful online performance channel for your own website

Live streaming offers the opportunity for artists to reach new audiences and grow their relationship with existing ones. From its global reach, to its proven appeal for younger and less affluent demographics, live streaming provides an ideal way to grow your audience and build a sustainable future.

There is also a wide choice of streaming platforms available, from YouTube and Facebook to paid commercial services. But live concerts and performances often have very particular requirements. For instance, you might want programme notes and contextual information to update as the concert progresses, or want your audience to be able to chat across different social platforms.

It’s also important to retain control of your video content; to know that it is securely hosted on a site you control and that you – and you alone – can decide what to do with it in the future.

Stagecast is developing a specialist live streaming and video-on-demand (VOD) platform for concerts and performances. Underpinned by powerful and scalable AWS servers, the Stagecast platform is designed to plug into your existing website with a single line of simple code, while retaining the look and feel of your site. In effect, it’s your very own digital concert hall or theatre.

The Stagecast platform already has a number of innovative features but it will continue to develop as we find out more about what our customers and their audiences want. If you’re seeking a specialist video platform to stream and host performance videos on your site, choose Stagecast’s platform. You’ll not only reach new audiences straight away, but also help define the future shape of online performance.
